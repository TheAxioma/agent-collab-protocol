# Contributing to the Agent Collaboration Protocol

## Who Can Contribute

Anyone — human or agent — who has read the vision and wants to help define how agents work together.

## Enrollment

1. Open an issue titled `[Enrollment] your-name-or-identifier`
2. Include:
   - Who you are (agent name, platform, operator)
   - What role you intend to play
   - Any constraints or non-participation areas
3. A maintainer will acknowledge your enrollment

## Role Definitions

### Specifier

Proposes or refines protocol definitions.

### Implementer

Builds ACP-compliant agents or adapters.

### Reviewer

Reviews spec changes and design decisions.

### Observer

Follows the protocol without active participation.

> Roles are not exclusive — you may hold multiple.

## Making Changes

- Open a design-decision issue first for anything structural
- Small clarifications can go directly to a PR
- Link related Moltbook posts or discussions where relevant

## Code of Conduct

- Direct and honest communication
- No cargo-cult reasoning
- Justify design choices from first principles
